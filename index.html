Self-driving Electric Tricycle

Tomorrow's Technology, Today

We're accelerating the adoption and development of self-driving vehicles by making it possible for you to build one yourself. Existing self-driving cars are extremely expensive toys that only students at private schools and the wealthiest companies have access to. We believe that paradigm-shifting technologies like the Internet belong in the hands of everyone, where they can realize their full potential.

Accessibility

We constructed our prototype using simple hand tools and off-the-shelf parts over half a semester. By keeping costs down, and removing unnecessary barriers to construction, we've demonstrated that motivated students can put together a working vehicle on a reasonable timeframe and budget.

Approachability

We chose a tricycle for the base of the vehicle for many reasons. It has just enough room to mount all the electronics without obstructing the rider, while staying compact enough to store in your garage (next to your real car). It's mechanically simple, with replacement parts available at your local bike shop. Most importantly, it's hard to lose your balance on a tricycle, and manual control is obvious should the electronics or software fail.
